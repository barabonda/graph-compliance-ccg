What is buddy bank?
A Shared Account Made for Real Life.
Buddy Bank lets groups pool money into one secure savings
account inside PPCBank Mobile. From family dinners to group trips, monthly budgeting, or community
activities, Buddy Bank makes shared money simple, safe, and stress-free.
Secure, Consent-driven, Transparency
Buddy Master:
Creates the account and manages member activities
Buddy Members:
Join, contribute, and request money take-out
Every action is transparent. Every member stays in control.

Key Features
One owner, everyone shares.
The Buddy Master owns the account. Members can see all
activity at any time.
Full transparency in every transaction.
No secrets, no confusion — all deposits, withdrawals, and
requests are visible to the whole group.
Collecting group money has never been easier.
Buddy Masters can send a Money Collection Request. Members
just tap to contribute.
Perfect for any group purpose.
Family dinners, friend trips, office team events, charity
groups, school committees — Buddy Bank fits every scenario.
Split bills like a pro.
Members can send a pay-back request to the Buddy Master for
shared expenses.
Join or leave anytime.
No paperwork. No hassle. Consent-based joining and leaving
with a single tap.
