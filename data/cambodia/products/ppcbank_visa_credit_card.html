PPCBANK VISA CREDIT CARD

Allows you to enjoy life’s pleasures with your spending!

PPCBank Visa Credit Card is an electronic card loaded with the latest technology. It makes transactions more flexible for your spending needs, enabling cash withdrawals worldwide from any ATM and payments via POS terminals.

You can access online payments easier and enjoy a wide range of benefits and discounts from our merchants and other promotions wherever Visa is accepted.

KEY BENEFITS

  • Welcome cash back up to 5%

  • Get cash back rewards on all purchases cross currency (Oversea POS purchase Transaction).

  • Receive free instant notifications for every transaction anywhere in the world

  • Tap and pay in seconds

  • Can be used domestically and internationally wherever Visa is accepted

  • Easy to manage on PPCBank mobile app, including card activation, card block, transaction management and more

  • Enjoy discount offers and promotions worldwide

REQUIREMENTS

  • Application must have a savings or current account with PPCBank

  • Valid ID card or Passport

CARD SECURITY

With PPCBank it is easy to make sure your card is secure. Please keep your card in a secure place. If you realize or suspect your card has been lost or stolen, please call our customer service hotline at 0963 909 909 / 023 909 909 or reach out through our live chat feature on the PPCBank Website.

After receiving the card:

  • Please sign the signature panel on the back of the card

  • You can active your new card on PPCBank Mobile App or ATM Machine

  • Keep your card in a secure place, if issue occurs will response by cardholder

PIN Usage

  • Please memorize your PIN, do not write it down

  • Do not let anyone use your card and do not disclose your PIN to anyone

  • Please change your original PIN to one of your choices on PPCBank Mobile app or at any PPCBank ATM.

  • Make sure your PIN is protected from being viewed when using an ATM

  • Please do not share card information, including full card number, expiry date, and security code (CVV Code) to anyone
